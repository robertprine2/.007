How to make a sandwich!

  1. Purchase bread, meat, condiments and set aside.

  2. Take plate, knife and set on counter next to bread, meat, and condiments.

  3. Take tie of of bread package by untieing it, and set aside the take two pieces out and gently place on plate.

  4. Slowy twist off lid for condiments and place knife in container then spread out evenly on each piece of bread. Repeating as neccessary. Set knife in sink and place lids back on condiments then put in fridge.

  5. Open packge of meat slowly and take x amount of pieces and put on bread repeating with cheese if necessary. Then close package and place in fridge.

  6. Lastly put slice of bread on top of meat and then consume with juice or chips.
